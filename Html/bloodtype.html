Blood Types and There Information

O-ve (O Negative)

Can Recieve From: O-ve

Reason for Compatibility:Universal donor for all blood types. No A/B antigens and no Rh antigen, making it compatible with any blood type.

Can Donate To: O-ve, O+ve, A-ve, A+ve, B-ve, B+ve, AB-ve, AB+ve

O+ve (O Positive)

Can Receive : O-ve, O+ve

Reason for Compatibility: No A/B antigens but has Rh+; can donate to Rh+ types and receive from O types.

Can Donate To: O+ve, A+ve, B+ve, AB+ve

A-ve (A Negative)

Can Receive From: A-ve, O-ve

Reason for Compatibility: Has A antigen and no Rh antigen; can donate to A and AB types, but only receives Rh- negative A or O blood.

Can Donate To: A-ve, A+ve, AB-ve, AB+ve

A+ve (A Positibe)

Can Receive From: A-ve, A+ve, O-ve, O+ve

Reason for Compatibility: Has A antigen and Rh+; can donate to Rh+ A and AB types, but can receive both Rh+ and Rh- A or O blood.

Can Donate To: A+ve, AB+ve

B-ve (B Negative)

Can Receive From: B-ve, O-ve

Can Donate To: B-ve, B+ve, AB-ve, AB+ve

Reason for Compatibility:Has B antigen and no Rh antigen; can donate to B and AB types, but only receives Rh- negative B or O blood.

B+ve (B Positive)

Can Receive From: B-ve, B+ve, O-ve, O+ve

Can Donate To: B+ve, AB+ve

Reason for Compatibility:Has B antigen and Rh+; can donate to Rh+ B and AB types, but can receive both Rh+ and Rh- B or O blood.

AB-ve (AB Negative)

Can Receive From: AB-ve, A-ve, B-ve, O-ve

Can Donate To: AB-ve, AB+ve

Reason for Compatibility:Universal donor for AB blood types, but can only receive from Rh- negative types (A-ve, B-ve, AB-ve, O-ve).

AB+ve (AB Positive)

Can Receive From: All blood types (O-ve, O+ve, A-ve, A+ve, B-ve, B+ve, AB-ve, AB+ve)

Can Donate To: AB+ve

Reason for Compatibility:Universal recipient; can receive from any blood type due to the presence of both A/B antigens and Rh+
